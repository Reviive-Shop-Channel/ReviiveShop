Titles You’ve Downloaded

To access a list of software titles you have downloaded, point at Titles You’ve Downloaded on the main menu of the Wii Shop Channel and press .
Here, you can redownload software that was deleted from your Wii console or download updated software for free.
Point at software titles that appear as Downloadable and press to view more information. Then, you can choose to select Download or Update on the Details screen.

*There may be cases in which a software title has been canceled or removed without prior notification. If this happens, you will not be able to redownload that title.
